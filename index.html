Hail Team

A group of specialists who never stop.

The Hail team is the Ballsystem staff that work on damage caused by atmospheric events. A machine in the machine, Hail Team works promptly on private cars as well as large fleet, thanks to a close monitoring of the weather conditions that enables to get ready and take action in the areas hit by bad weather.

In a time of environmental crisis, with a dramatic increase in precipitation like sudden hailstorms, the Ballsystem Hail Team offers a reliable solution, fast and safe, working together with insurance companies for a global assistance of the customer.
